Workplace Wellbeing is the Foundation of Your Business

Micro-stress is the Health Epidemic of the 21st Century costing companies 12bn working days and $1tn p.a. in lost productivity globally. Beam ‘Connects the Dots’ to understand the inter-related factors causing micro-stress that Employees and Employers often can’t see. Beam minimises the impact of micro stress and helps mitigate its cost to organisations
