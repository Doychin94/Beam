FAQ

How does Beam work?

Beam doesn’t believe in work-life balance. Work is just another part of life. Beam is dedicated to improving user’s insights holistically, inside and outside of the office.

Here at Beam we are focused on improving our user’s wellness for years to come. That’s why we proudly support the Wellness 2050 initiative to build a better more mentally conscious workplace. We seek to work with proactive business leaders who wish to push beyond work-life balance.
